# Sip or Slip: The Bronze Age Bar

Sip or Slip is a short browser game that teaches players about ancient Chinese bronze vessels through a simulated drinking ritual. Choose a social occasion, pick the right vessels step by step, and see how your ceremony unfolds — either with elegance or complete social disaster.

---

## Why This Game Exists

Ancient Chinese bronze culture is rich, visual, and deeply tied to social rules, but it is often presented as static museum information. For most Western audiences, it feels distant and hard to relate to.

This project started from a simple question: what if instead of reading about ritual rules, you had to perform them?

By turning historical etiquette into a game of choices and consequences, Sip or Slip makes players feel the pressure, hierarchy, and humor of ancient ceremonies. Instead of memorizing facts, players learn by making mistakes.

---

## How It Works

Players begin by choosing a social occasion, ranging from a formal state dinner to a casual backyard gathering. Each occasion maps to a hidden social rank.

The game then unfolds in three steps:
1. Choose a storage vessel
2. Choose a warming vessel
3. Choose a drinking vessel

Each choice affects the outcome. Correct selections lead to a smooth and visually satisfying ceremony. Wrong choices trigger humorous animations, spills, and social consequences.

At the end, players watch a short cinematic of their ceremony, followed by a result screen that explains what happened, reveals historical facts, and presents a short narrative based on their performance.

---

## Core Features

- Three distinct occasions with different social expectations and outcomes
- Three-step decision system: storage, warming, and drinking vessels
- Illustrated vessel cards with Chinese name, pinyin, and English translation
- Wine Meter that visually responds to the selected drinking vessel
- Cinematic sequence showing the consequences of player choices
- Multiple outcome tiers: Perfect Ceremony, Minor Breach, Total Disgrace
- Pre-written story system with dynamic selection based on player performance
- Vessel collection system that tracks discoveries across playthroughs
